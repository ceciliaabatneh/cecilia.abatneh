Inventions in Japan

ROBOT

The population of Japan is aging at a faster pace than any other country. Light manufacturing, regenerative medicine, autonomous driving technology, and home robotics. The machines that have been created are able to create solutions to problems without any help from humans, and in the future, they will even be able to talk to each other. These robots can take to place of top-level doctors in regenerative medicine. “Japan will break new ground for the future of humankind.” -Masayo Takahashi, M.D., Ph. D. This relates back to technological inventions and innovations because Japan created this incredible technological invention - the robot - to help them construct even more innovations.
